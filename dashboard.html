Help Center

Getting Started

Welcome to the Student Attendance Portal. This comprehensive system helps you track and manage your attendance across all subjects.

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides a quick overview of your attendance statistics, including daily attendance records and subject-wise breakdown.

Attendance Tracking

Your attendance is automatically recorded for each class. The system uses advanced tracking to ensure accuracy and reliability.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my attendance percentage calculated?

Attendance is calculated as the ratio of classes attended to total classes held, expressed as a percentage.

What should I do if I notice an attendance discrepancy?

Use the query form in the dashboard to report any discrepancies. Include the date and subject details.

What is the minimum attendance requirement?

A minimum of 75% attendance is required in each subject to be eligible for examinations.
